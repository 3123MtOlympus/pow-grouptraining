Welcome to our outdoor total body weight calisthenics exercise class! Participants can expect a dynamic and engaging workout that incorporates running, jumping, and various bodyweight exercises designed to improve strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Stay hydrated and come ready to challenge yourself in a supportive environment!
