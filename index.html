Join PerkzofWerk Calisthenics Group Classes

Welcome to our outdoor total body weight calisthenics exercise class! Participants can expect a dynamic and engaging workout that incorporates running, jumping, and various bodyweight exercises designed to improve strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Occasionally, we will use equipment to enhance our training, so be prepared for a variety of movements. To ensure a successful and enjoyable experience, attendees are required to bring their own water, a towel, and a mat for comfort during floor exercises. Stay hydrated and come ready to challenge yourself in a supportive environment!
